Integration Overview

Connecting Odoo ERP with Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis for economic development in Guatemala

This integration solution provides a comprehensive framework for connecting Odoo ERP with Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA) systems to support site selection, business model structuring, and portfolio management for economic development in Guatemala. The solution leverages URBOP's existing methodologies and data sources while providing a unified operational platform through Odoo ERP.

Key Features

Site Selection

Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis for evaluating and ranking potential development sites based on economic, social, environmental, technical, and legal criteria.

Business Model Canvas

Structured approach to business model development with templates for different asset classes and integration with market research data.

Portfolio Management

Comprehensive tracking and analysis of real estate assets with performance metrics, categorization, and reporting capabilities.

Data Integration

Seamless connection with the Guatemala Data Hub for geographic and demographic information to support evidence-based decision making.
